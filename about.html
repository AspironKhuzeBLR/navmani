About Us

Trusted By 200 + satisfied clients

At NAVMANI, Established in 2020, Navamani draws inspiration from the meaning of its name—Nine Beads—believed to offer protection, positive energy, and emotional balance. We specialize in traditional imitation jewelry and accessories that reflect cultural richness and timeless elegance. Every piece is crafted with care to blend beauty and meaning. With a commitment to quality and style, we proudly serve customers across India.

Navamani, established in 2021, is a jewelry brand inspired by the power of nine beads, offering beautifully crafted imitation jewelry and accessories. Blending tradition with elegance, we customize pearl and bead pieces and deliver across India and abroad.

Our Mission

At Navamani, our mission is to celebrate and preserve the beauty of traditional jewelry while making it accessible to everyone. We are committed to crafting meaningful, high-quality imitation jewelry that reflects cultural heritage, promotes positive energy, and meets the unique style of every individual. Through customized designs and reliable delivery across India and abroad, we aim to connect hearts with heritage—one bead at a time.

Our Vision

Navamani envisions becoming a trusted global name in traditional imitation jewelry, where cultural significance meets contemporary style. We strive to inspire confidence and elegance through our handcrafted pieces, promoting the timeless value of tradition while continuously innovating in design and service to reach hearts across the world.

Navamani—where tradition meets timeless elegance through the power of nine sacred beads.
